# Business Model

## Value Proposition

AI.me provides a graph-based metadata infrastructure that makes AI agents reliable, auditable, and aligned with reality. We solve the critical problem of AI hallucinations and lack of traceability in enterprise AI deployments.

## Target Market

### Primary Segments

1. **Enterprise Knowledge Management**
   - Large organizations with extensive documentation
   - Need for searchable, queryable knowledge bases
   - Compliance requirements

2. **Regulated Industries**
   - Financial services
   - Healthcare
   - Government
   - Legal

3. **Customer Support Automation**
   - Companies deploying AI agents
   - Need for accurate, traceable responses

## Revenue Model

### SaaS Licensing
- Subscription-based pricing
- Tiered by usage (documents, queries)
- Enterprise plans with custom features

### API Consumption
- Pay-per-use model
- Volume discounts
- Enterprise contracts

### Enterprise Deployments
- On-premise deployments
- Custom integrations
- Professional services

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Technical Moat**: Graph-based reasoning difficult to replicate
2. **AWS Partnership**: Strategic integration with AWS services
3. **Compliance Focus**: Built-in audit trails and traceability
4. **Agent Alignment**: Infrastructure for future of agentic AI
