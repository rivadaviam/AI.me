# Market Analysis

## Market Size

The AI agent market is growing exponentially. Organizations are adopting AI agents but face challenges with reliability and compliance.

## Market Trends

1. **Rapid AI Adoption**: Enterprises rapidly adopting AI
2. **Compliance Requirements**: Increasing regulatory requirements
3. **Agentic AI Growth**: Shift toward autonomous agents
4. **Groundedness Demand**: Need for reliable AI responses
