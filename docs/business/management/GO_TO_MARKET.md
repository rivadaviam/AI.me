# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Market Entry

### Phase 1: Early Adopters
- Target: Tech-forward enterprises
- Channels: Direct sales, AWS Marketplace
- Focus: Product validation

### Phase 2: Market Expansion
- Target: Regulated industries
- Channels: Partners, direct sales
- Focus: Compliance use cases

### Phase 3: Scale
- Target: Broad enterprise market
- Channels: Multiple channels
- Focus: Market leadership

## Marketing Strategy

- Content marketing
- Technical blog posts
- Case studies
- Conference presentations
- AWS re:Invent

## Sales Strategy

- Direct sales for enterprise
- Self-service for SMB
- Partner channel
- AWS Marketplace
