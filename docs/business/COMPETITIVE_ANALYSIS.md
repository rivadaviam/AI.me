# Competitive Analysis

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

Limited direct competitors in graph-based metadata infrastructure for AI agents.

### Indirect Competitors

1. **Vector Databases**: Pinecone, Weaviate
   - Different approach (vectors vs graphs)
   - We offer reasoning and validation

2. **Knowledge Bases**: Notion AI, Confluence
   - Different focus (collaboration vs AI infrastructure)
   - We focus on AI agent integration

3. **LLM Providers**: OpenAI, Anthropic
   - We complement, not compete
   - We add groundedness and auditability

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Graph-Based Reasoning**: Unique approach to knowledge representation
2. **Validation Engine**: Built-in groundedness validation
3. **Audit Trail**: Complete traceability
4. **AWS Integration**: Strategic partnership

## Differentiation

- Focus on agentic AI infrastructure
- Compliance-first design
- Graph-based reasoning
- AWS ecosystem integration
